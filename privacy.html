Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 7, 2025

1. General Information

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, store it, and protect it.

2. Data We Collect

  • Photos and images: The images you add are stored locally on your device in the path defined by the app.
  • Photo metadata: Title, selected widget size, record identifier, and file path.
  • Widget settings: Parameters you select, such as which photo to display.
  • System data (non-personalized): Technical information required for the widget to function correctly (OS version, widget identifiers, etc.).

We do not collect accounts, contact information, payment data, or location data.

3. How We Use the Data

  • To display your selected images in the widget.
  • To save settings so the widget updates correctly.
  • To support app features such as resizing, widget updates, and photo selection.

We do not use data for marketing, targeted advertising, or behavioral analytics.

4. Storage Location and Duration

All data is stored locally on your device and within the shared container group.com.dostan.widgetCreator, accessible only to the app and widget.

Data is deleted when you uninstall the app or manually delete photos or settings. We do not transfer any data to external servers or sync with the cloud, unless you enable iCloud or device backup manually.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Data processing is based on your consent, expressed by installing and using the app. You may withdraw your consent at any time by stopping use, deleting your data, or uninstalling the app.

6. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your data with third parties. Exceptions apply only when required by law (e.g., court orders or law enforcement requests).

7. Security

We use Apple’s standard security mechanisms (sandbox, App Group). We recommend enabling Face ID, Touch ID, or a device passcode and managing photo access permissions carefully.

8. User Rights

  • View, modify, and delete added images and settings.
  • Restrict or deny photo access in iOS/iPadOS settings.
  • Contact the developer with data-related questions.

If you are in a jurisdiction with additional privacy rights (e.g., GDPR), you may fully exercise those rights.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when releasing new features. Updates will be reflected in the app and/or on our website. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@createwidget.app.
